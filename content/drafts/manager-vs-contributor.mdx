One of the biggest challenges one faces as an early-stage startup founders is managing two modes of thinking: managing and operating.

The challenge for most startup founders is that they are so used to operating that they don’t know how to manage. They don’t know how to delegate, how to set up systems, how to hire and fire, how to manage cash flow, how to manage people, how to manage time, how to manage themselves.

On the flip side, you have some folks who are great managers but don’t know how to operate. They don’t know how to get their hands dirty, how to build a product, how to sell, how to market, how to do customer support, how to do the things that are necessary to get a business off the ground.
